# ValidateCall Pricing Calculations

## Base Costs (Vapi)
- Average call duration: 5 minutes
- Vapi cost per minute: ~$0.10
- **Base cost per call: $0.50**

## Pricing Strategy with Margins (Psychological Pricing Applied)

### Lite Plan (75% Margin)
- Cost per call: $0.50
- Target price per call: $2.00
- Monthly package: 100 calls = **$197/month** (psychological pricing)
- Actual margin: 74.6%
- Profit: $147/month

### Starter Plan (50% Margin)
- Cost per call: $0.50
- Target price per call: $1.00
- Monthly package: 500 calls = **$497/month** (psychological pricing)
- Actual margin: 49.7%
- Profit: $247/month
- Popular tier for small businesses

### Pro Plan (25% Margin)
- Cost per call: $0.50
- Target price per call: $0.67
- Monthly package: 2000 calls = **$1,337/month** (psychological pricing - LEET!)
- Actual margin: 25.2%
- Profit: $337/month
- Best value for enterprises

## Psychological Pricing Benefits
- Prices ending in 7 or 9 increase conversion rates by 5-15%
- $197 feels significantly cheaper than $200
- $497 feels like under $500 (major psychological barrier)
- $1,337 is memorable (LEET speak) and feels like $1,300 range instead of $1,400
